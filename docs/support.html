Frequently Asked Questions

How do I log in to the app?

Use your CTICU credentials provided to you. Enter your username and password on the login screen.

How do I view the schedule?

After logging in, the schedule tab shows the current rotation schedule. You can scroll through dates and filter by different views.

Is my data secure?

Yes, all data is transmitted securely using HTTPS encryption. Your login credentials are stored securely on your device.

Which devices are supported?

CTICU Schedule is optimized for iPhone devices running iOS 13.4 or later.
